Privacy Policy

SimpleSplit
Last Updated: January 2026
In short: SimpleSplit does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your inputs remain exclusively on your device.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "SimpleSplit" (hereinafter "App"), developed and provided by Arne Lange (hereinafter "Developer" or "we").

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This app was developed with the principle of "Privacy by Design" and deliberately does not collect any data.

2. What Data is Collected?

None.

SimpleSplit does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data or usage information. The app works completely offline and locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

All data you enter into the app (e.g., names, expense amounts, calculations) is stored exclusively locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to servers
  • Is not shared with the developer
  • Is not passed on to third parties
  • Is accessible only to you

4. Permissions

The app requires no special permissions. It does not request access to:

  • Internet/Network
  • Location
  • Camera
  • Contacts
  • Files
  • Or other sensitive device data

5. Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

Since no data is collected, no data can be shared with third parties. The app contains:

  • No advertising
  • No tracking tools
  • No analytics/statistics services
  • No social media integration
  • No cloud synchronization

6. Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies and does not perform tracking. No information about your usage behavior is collected or analyzed.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored exclusively locally on your device, the security of this data is your responsibility. We recommend:

  • Protecting your device with a password/PIN
  • Performing regular backups of your device
  • Note that app data will be deleted when the app is uninstalled

8. User Rights (GDPR)

Since no personal data is collected, most GDPR rights are not applicable. However, you theoretically have the following rights:

  • Right to access: There is no data about you
  • Right to deletion: Simply uninstall the app
  • Right to object: Not relevant, as no data processing occurs
  • Data portability: Not applicable

9. Children

The app is suitable for all age groups. Since no data is collected, there are no special provisions for children under 13 years of age.

10. Google Play Store

The app is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain information about downloads and usage as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This data is collected by Google, not by us.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be published on this page. For significant changes, we will inform you through the app.
